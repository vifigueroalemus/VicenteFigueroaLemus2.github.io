IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Case Study

Talent management and employee retention have become central focuses of my professional interest. In today's competitive business environment, the ability to attract and, above all, retain valuable employees is more critical than ever. To go deeper into these dynamics, I decided to conduct an exploratory analysis using Excel, Power BI, and pandas (Python library), working with the public Kaggle dataset "IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance." The fundamental purpose of this article is to present the key findings derived from my comprehensive analysis of this dataset, with a specific focus on unraveling the factors that have the most significant impact on employee turnover.

Strategic Regional Performance Analysis: An Excel Case Study for Schamberger-Rowe

Schamberger-Rowe, a global enterprise operating across four regions (NAM, EMEA, APAC, LATAM), faced challenges while interpreting Q2 2021 volume fluctuations. Through a comprehensive Excel analysis, I identified: 1) Critical slowdown in LATAM (-7k units due to loss of strategic clients) 2) Opportunity in APAC (+2.3% growth with a reduced client base) 3) 80% automation of manual reporting processes
